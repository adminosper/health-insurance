Indian Health Insurance Domain Guide

1. Plan (The Blueprint / Product)
   - Static template defining rules, limits, and exclusions. 

   A. Plan Identity & Options:
      - plan_id: Unique identifier for the product (e.g., "Optima Secure").
      - allowed_sum_insured_options: List of available coverage options (e.g., [₹5 Lakhs, ₹10 Lakhs, ₹20 Lakhs]).
      - allowed_premium_frequencies: List of allowed payment intervals (e.g., [Monthly, Quarterly, Annually]).
      - premium_calculation_logic: Matrix/formula to compute premium based on age, zone, family size, chosen sum insured, and frequency (OUT OF SCOPE).

   B. Network Rules:
      - network_list: Allowed hospitals for cashless claims.

   C. Enhancement Rules (Templates):
      - ncb_rule: Rate of bonus increase per claim-free year (e.g., +10%/year, max 50%).
      - restore_benefit_rule: Conditions to restore Sum Insured (e.g., restore 100% on exhaustion for unrelated illness).
      - pre_post_hospitalization_rule: Number of covered days before (e.g., 30) & after (e.g., 60) admission.
      - ayush_cover_rule: Eligibility and limit for alternative treatments.

   D. Exclusion Rules (Time-Based & Permanent):
      - initial_waiting_period: 30 days (accidents excluded).
      - specific_disease_waiting: 1-2 years for listed conditions (e.g., hernia).
      - pre_existing_disease (ped) waiting: 2-4 years.
      - standard_exclusions: Services never covered (cosmetics, diagnostics, self-harm).
      - consumables_exclusion: List of non-medical billing items (gloves, PPE) always deducted.

   E. Capping Rules (Limits):
      - room_rent_capping: Room/ICU limits; exceeding triggers proportionate deductions.
      - disease_sublimit: Max payout caps for specific treatments (e.g., Cataract).
      - domiciliary_hospitalization: Cap for home-care treatments.

   F. Cost-Sharing Rules (Templates):
      - co_payment_rule: Standard % member pays.
      - deductible_rule: Out-of-pocket threshold before plan pays.

2. Policy Contract (The Instance / Enrollment)
   - Stateful contract generated per customer purchase.
   - policy_id: Unique contract identifier.
   - plan_id: Reference to the Plan Blueprint.
   - tenure: Start/end dates (30-day renewal grace period).
   - chosen_sum_insured: Base annual coverage chosen from Plan's options.
   - chosen_frequency: Payment interval chosen from Plan's options.
   - premium_amount: The recurring payment amount derived at enrollment (OUT OF SCOPE).
   
   - policyholder_details: (The primary contract owner)
      - policyholder_name: Legal name of the owner.
      - contact_info: Mobile number and email.
      - kyc_details: PAN/Aadhaar (required for high-value payouts/refunds).
      - bank_details: (Where reimbursement payouts are sent)
         - account_holder_name: Legal name on bank account.
         - bank_name: e.g., ICICI Bank.
         - account_number: Bank account number.
         - ifsc_code: Standard Indian branch routing code.

   - stateful_accumulators:
      - available_sum_insured: Remaining cover (Base + NCB - Paid Claims).
      - accumulated_ncb: Current earned No Claim Bonus amount.
      - active_deductible_paid: Deductible paid by member so far this year.
      - claims_history: List of claims processed (used to check NCB eligibility and restore triggers).
      - days_active: Duration active (to compute waiting period status).

3. Member / Insured Individual
   - The individuals covered under a Policy (family floater list).
   - member_id: Unique identifier for the insured person.
   - policy_id: Reference to the Policy Contract.
   - full_name: Customer's legal name.
   - date_of_birth: Used to calculate age during adjudication (waiting periods, limits).
   - gender: Male / Female / Other.
   - relationship: Relationship to primary policyholder (Self, Spouse, Child, Parent).
   - pre_existing_diseases (ped): List of medical conditions declared at purchase (triggers PED waiting periods).

4. Customer Claim Flows
   - The two ways members process claims under the policy contract.

   A. Cashless Flow (At Network Hospital)
      - Phase 1: Pre-Authorization (Admission)
         - hospital_desk: Collects member card & doctor's diagnosis/cost estimate.
         - submission: Sends Pre-Auth request form to Insurer/TPA.
         - insurer_checks: Verifies policy status, network eligibility, exclusions, and active sum insured.
         - initial_approval: Insurer issues a temporary coverage cap letter (ETA: 2-3 hours).
         - denial_path: If denied, patient pays out-of-pocket and files for reimbursement later.
      - Phase 2: Final Adjudication (Discharge)
         - final_bill_submission: Hospital submits final itemized bill & discharge summary.
         - rule_execution: Insurer runs capping, cost-sharing, and permanent exclusion rules on each bill item.
         - final_approval: Insurer settles approved amount directly with the hospital (ETA: 3-4 hours).
         - patient_liability: Member pays excluded non-medical items (consumables) + copays directly to hospital.

   B. Reimbursement Flow (At Non-Network Hospital)
      - Phase 1: Discharge & Out-of-Pocket Payment
         - member_action: Pays total hospital bill, collects original invoices, discharge summary, reports, and receipts.
      - Phase 2: Document Submission
         - submission: Customer uploads/submits all original documents + claim form within filing deadline (e.g., 30 days).
         - mandatory_documents:
            - signed_claim_form: Part A (member) and Part B (hospital stamp).
            - discharge_summary: Explains diagnosis, medical history, and treatment.
            - itemized_final_bill: Granular breakdown of hospital charges.
            - original_payment_receipts: Proof of out-of-pocket bill settlement.
            - pharmacy_bills_prescriptions: Supporting bills for claimed medicines.
            - diagnostic_reports: Lab/imaging results justifying the treatment.
            - cancelled_cheque: Verification for NEFT payout.
      - Phase 3: Manual Auditing & Verification
         - audit: Insurer/TPA reviews documents. Missing docs trigger "Query Raised" status.
      - Phase 4: Final Settlement
         - rule_execution: Insurer runs the same capping, cost-sharing, and exclusion rules.
         - payout: Insurer transfers approved amount to policyholder's bank details (ETA: 15-30 days).

   C. Cashless vs. Reimbursement System Differentiation
      - channel: Cashless is submitted via Partner Provider Portal; Reimbursement via Customer App.
      - payee: Cashless pays the Hospital; Reimbursement pays the Policyholder.
      - pre_auth_link: Cashless requires a linked `pre_auth_id`; Reimbursement has no pre-auth link.

   D. Claim Dispute Flow (Appeals)
      - customer_action: Initiates appeal via App within dispute window (e.g., 90 days from EOB) with supporting proof.
      - dispute_status: Claim status changes to `Disputed`.
      - review: Independent senior medical officer manually re-adjudicates the claim.
      - resolution:
         - dispute_upheld: Additional amount approved $\rightarrow$ top-up payment processed.
         - dispute_rejected: Original decision stands $\rightarrow$ detailed explanation sent.
         - escalation: Customer can escalate to the Insurance Ombudsman if unresolved.

5. Claim Entity (The Adjudication Object)
   - Submitted transaction representing the hospital invoice and adjudication outcome.

   A. Claim Metadata (Header):
      - claim_id: Unique identifier for the claim.
      - policy_id: Policy contract being claimed against.
      - member_id: Specific patient receiving treatment.
      - hospital_id: Treating hospital.
      - network_status: Cashless (Network) vs. Reimbursement (Non-Network).
      - tenure_details: admission_date and discharge_date.
      - diagnosis_code: Primary clinical code (e.g., ICD-10) for waiting period and sub-limit checks.
      - claim_status: (Submitted, Under Review, Approved, Denied, Paid, Disputed).

   B. Line Items (Invoice Details):
      - line_item_id: Unique billing item ID.
      - service_category: Category (e.g., Room Rent, ICU Charges, Doctor Consultation, OT Charges, Medicines, Consumables).
      - billed_amount: Original hospital charge.

   C. Adjudication Outputs (Per Line Item):
      - allowed_amount: Billed amount minus capping limits.
      - disallowed_amount: Portion rejected.
      - copay_deducted: Member's copay share.
      - insurer_payable: Final amount the insurance company pays.
      - adjudication_status: (Approved, Denied, Needs Review).
      - explanation: specific reason text (e.g., "Denied: Consumables are non-payable").

   D. Claim-Level Aggregates (Rollups):
      - total_billed: Sum of line item billed amounts.
      - total_allowed: Sum of allowed amounts.
      - total_insurer_payable: Total payable by insurer.
      - total_member_payable: Total member share (disallowed + copays + deductibles).

   E. Dispute & Audit:
      - is_disputed: Boolean flag for member appeal.
      - dispute_reason: Customer-submitted text explanation.
      - dispute_status: (Open, Resolved, Rejected).
      - adjuster_notes: Internal medical review comments.
